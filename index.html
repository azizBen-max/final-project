Politics

The field of politics has undergone significant transformations in recent decades. The end of the Cold War in the early 1990s reshaped global geopolitics, leading to shifts in alliances and international relations. The rise of social media and digital communication platforms in the 2000s transformed political campaigning and public engagement, amplifying both grassroots movements and disinformation campaigns.

Gaming

The gaming industry has evolved rapidly over the decades, beginning with early arcade games like Pong in the 1970s. The introduction of home consoles such as the Atari 2600 in the late 1970s revolutionized gaming accessibility. The 1990s saw the rise of 3D graphics with consoles like the PlayStation, while the 2000s brought online multiplayer gaming to the forefront with platforms like Xbox Live and Steam, transforming how games are played and experienced globally.
